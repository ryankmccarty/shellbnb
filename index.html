FAQs

Booking expand_more
How do I reserve a shell? expand_more

Just search your destination, choose a cozy spot, and tap “Book My Shell.” We’ll take care of the rest.

Do I need to leave a deposit? expand_more

Some hosts request a clam deposit. You’ll see this before checkout — no surprises.

Can I cancel my booking? expand_more

Yes! Most shells offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before your stay begins.

Stays expand_more
Are hosts usually on-site? expand_more

Some are! Others give you full privacy. Each listing notes whether the host is pond-side or remote.

Can I bring other turtles with me? expand_more

Definitely — as long as the listing allows multiple guests. Look for the “Turtle Group Friendly” tag.

Is bedding (like seaweed or moss) provided? expand_more

Most listings include bedding, but double-check the amenities before you book.

Still have questions? Contact us!
